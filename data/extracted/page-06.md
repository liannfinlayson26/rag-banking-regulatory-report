## Narrative

Key metrics
The table below sets out the key regulatory metrics covering the Group's available capital (including buffer requirements and ratios), RWAs,
leverage ratio, LCR and NSFR. Effective 1 January 2025, the IFRS 9 transitional arrangements came to an end, followed by the end of the CRR II
grandfathering provisions on 28 June 2025. Accordingly, our capital figures are the same on both the transitional and end-point basis. The
leverage ratio is calculated using the CRR II end-point basis for capital. LCR is reported as the average of the preceding 12 months while NSFR
is reported as the average of the preceding four quarter-end values.
